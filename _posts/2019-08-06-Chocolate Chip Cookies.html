Ninja Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Ninja Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe. Makes 15-20 yummy cookies!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup + 2 Tbsp Flower
  • 1/2 Tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/2 Tsp Salt
  • 1/2 Cup (1) Stick of Butter
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/2 Tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1/4 Cup + 2 Tbsp Granulated Sugar
  • 1/4 Cup + 2 Tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 3/4 Cup Chocolate Chips

Directions

  1. Pre-Heat Oven to 350°F
  2. In a separate bowl combine the Flower, Baking Soda, and Salt
  3. Using the Dough Blade combine the Butter, Egg, Vanilla Extract, White and Brown Sugar
    • Blend 30 seconds, Scrape the sides, Add Flower/Baking Soda/Salt mixture
    • Pulse 5x times, then scrape the sides
    • Blend 30 seconds
  4. Remove the Dough Blade, add the Chocolate Chips, mix with a spoon
  5. Place 1 TBSP globs of Cookie Dough on Baking Sheet and Bake for 8 min at 350°F
  6. Edges should be golden brown and the center should be soft, but not raw. Add an additional min as needed until desired result is achieved.
    Remove from baking sheet and place foil on cool counter to cool
  7. Once cooled, removed from foil and place on serving tray. Cover with foil to keep fresh
